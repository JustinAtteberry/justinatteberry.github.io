Physical Strength & Anxiety Data Analysis

I found this dataset from Kaggle and decided to look at the correlation between the strength of the tested individuals and their anxiety scores (HEXACO IPIP 7-point scale). I used SQL to clean the data into an easier to read format, then used SQL again to analize the data and create tables to be used in a Jupyter Notebook to look at their correlation. Looking at the heatmap in the Python Correlation Analysis, there is a high correlation between sex and grip/chest strength. There is also a high correlation between grip strength and chest strength. There is a slight correlation between all positively keyed anxiety scores. Finally, there is barely some correlation between all negatively keyed anxiety scores.
