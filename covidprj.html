COVID-19 Data Analysis

Using the data from Our World in Data, I then split the data into two tables, one for Deaths and one for Vaccinations. I then used SQL to analyze the data and look at different percentages such as death percentage, contraction percentage, and population infected percentage. Using some of the SQL queries, I then created a Tableau Viz to visualize the tables and even look at some possible projections for the future of infection rates for some countries.
